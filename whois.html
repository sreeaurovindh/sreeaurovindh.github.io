Who is Sree Aurovindh?

passionate, and aspiring data scientist...


Sree Aurovindh Viswanathan is the Computer Science Doctoral Student in the Ira A. Fulton Schools of Engineering at Arizona State University. My research work focuses on development of machine learning models to understand human collaborative behavior. My expertise is on the intersection of machine learning, speech processing and large scale data analytics. I received my M.S degree from ASU in Computer science in 2014.Previously, I pursued my research in software development with Software Engineering and Technology labs (SET Labs) at Infosys, Bangalore. I have published four papers on conferences and referred Journal publications including IEEE Transactions on Learning technologies and have one patent..

  • Analyzing enormous volumes of Unstructured Datasets
  • Statistical Modeling
  • Predictive Modeling and Machine Learning
  • Big data Technologies
  • Data Visualization
  • Scientific Python and R Stack
  • Programming Languages (Python and Java)
