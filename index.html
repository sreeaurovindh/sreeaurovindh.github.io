Who is Sree Aurovindh?

Sree Aurovindh is the Computer Science Doctoral Student at Arizona State University. His interests involve predictive analytics, experimental analysis and design, big data technologies and data visualization.

Research

My research work focuses on development of machine learning models to understand human collaborative behavior. My expertise is on the intersection of machine learning, metric development, speech processing and large scale data analytics.
